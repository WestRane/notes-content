## A great advertisement
*March 2026*

This is how advertisements should basically be created. The company gives money to the people who can insert the advertising material into their product in the most natural way possible. And that's exactly how this short feels.

The three-wheeler bike is definitely an interesting option for a camper. But do I really care about whether Rin uses a bike with two, three or a hundred wheels? Of course I do! And the key reason for such a reaction of mine is the details. Three wheels help Rin drive through leaves, small branches and stuff. The powerful engine - compared to her usual one - lets her drive faster, but the cold bites at her much faster too. You see? 

It's not just "Hey, this bike could be useful for you, buy it now!", it's more like "Let's make a nice short with the main character riding a new bike in the anime that already featured the bike theme, and maybe the viewer would get interested in the bike too". Rin is a beloved character by many people who love this franchise. Her internal monologues help the viewer to consider whether the third wheel is useful and so forth. But what's even more important: these monologues are consistent with her character. It feels so natural being so "Yuru Camp"-ish, while also making a solid collaboration.

This is the advertisement where the character has a realistic motivation to swap her bike for a moment. The advertisement where you can still feel the whole vibe of your favorite franchise, but with a touch of sponsorship that made Rin replace her usual bike with the three-wheeler one. The visuals are as beautiful as always, the music is simply great, and her ride to the sauna is interesting and very soothing. Isn't it lovely?
